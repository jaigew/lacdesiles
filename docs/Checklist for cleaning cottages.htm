Cottage Cleaning

 

It is essential that all renters arrive to a clean and neat cottage.  Our policy is that all renters leave the cottage at least as nice as you find it. As you know, there is no one to clean up after you. Upon completion of your stay, please use the following checklist as a guide for what needs to be done (at a minimum):

 

q  Sweep and, where possible, vacuum, the entire house on all floors (this includes moving beds and other furniture, where necessary), and then replace vacuum bag.

q  Vacuum or shake out all large carpets (and make sure to leave vacuum with empty bag).

q  Mop or hand-wash the floors in kitchen and bathrooms (including shower walls and floor).

q  Scrub all bathroom and kitchen counters after removing items from them to ensure that the entire surface gets well cleaned.

q  Scrub and clean stove, oven, refrigerator, and table/s.

q  Wash and neatly replace all dishes.

q  Wash, dry, fold, and replace all towels, sheets, and other household items that have been used.

q  Sweep all porches.

q  Replace broken boards on walkways and deck/s, or if not able, call Jean-Marc Duval to help, and also let manager know.

q  Tidy up around fireplace but please do not remove ashes (for fire safety), or if fireplace absolutely full, remove only cold-to-touch ashes to the metal garbage can in the garage.

q  Make sure kindling and firewood supplies are plentiful inside cottage.

q  Straighten up the wharf, bail and clean canoe/s and rowboat.

q  Leave canoe/s upturned on wharf and please leave paddles in the shade so they don’t warp.

q  Take recyclables, compost, and trash to respective bins at end of the Rue Daviault dirt road.

q  Tidy up around the cottage and trails; please make sure no personal items remain.

q  Leave cottage unlocked unless you are the last renter for the season.

 

Remember, if you are not able or prepared to spend the necessary amount of time cleaning or would just prefer not to spend precious vacation hours cleaning, the option always exists to hire Nathalie Charronl (number on phone list) who, for approximately $80 Canadian, will thoroughly clean the cottage (washing linens, etc. is extra). Everyone should be able to enjoy the start of their vacation by arriving to a clean and tidy cottage. Please alert the manager if you find a cottage in less than ideal condition.  Thank you!
