  • Entrelacs - tiny village about 3miles/5km from the cottages. Can also paddle a couple of miles to town wharf and walk .5 mile. Grocery store, convenient store, gas, church, cafe, hardware store, bank, and that's about it. One quite good restaurant, La Maison Bleue d'Entrelacs, that has a prix fixe menu. Town website includes local weather forecast.
    • Ste-Marguerite-du-Lac-Masson - small village, which includes a very nice restaurant, Bistro Champlain, with an internationally-acclaimed wine cellar. About 15 minutes away.
  • Val-David - a small village and nearby artists' community, with several nice restaurants and bakeries. It is also a gateway to the Linear Park, with rental facilities for biking, canoeing and kayaking. About 20-25 minutes away.
  • Ste Adele - larger town with several good restaurants, shopping, and cinema. About 20-25 minutes away.
  • "Le P'tit Train du Nord" Linear Park - 200km trail on old railroad bed that follows rivers, lakes, and rugged landscapes, and with some places to eat/drink along the way. Great for biking, walking, xc skiing. Trail access points about 25 minutes away.
  • Saint-Saveur - a larger more built up village with a wide variety of restaurants, water and alpine slides, other sports. In winter there are lots of downhill ski slopes. In fact, the area's claim to fame is the largest illuminated ski area in the world that is about 25-30 minutes away.
  • Montreal - wonderful, cosmopolitan city with all sorts of opportunities including various festivals all summer. Many other websites with info on Montreal to check out, such as Wikipedia, Wikitravel. About one hour's drive.
  • Lanaudiere region - Lac des Iles is exactly on the line between these two regions: Lanaudiere and Laurentians.
  • Laurentian region - more well known region than Lanaudiere and more developed.
  • Mont-Tremblant National Park - great outdoor adventures in largest protected area in Quebec, with moose and other wildlife. About an hour away.
  • Bonjour Quebec - tourist site for Government of Quebec.
Beyond an hour's drive:
  • Ottawa - Capital of Canada. About 2.5 hours away.
  • Quebec City - Capital of Quebec. About 3.5 hours away.
