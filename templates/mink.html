Mink Point

The cottage, located on a small peninsula surrounded on three sides by the lake, was built in 1970. The property is approximately an acre in size and has some lovely trails.

The cottage has accommodations for a family of up to five people. Furnishings are simple but adequate. The cottage is equipped with blankets, but family and friends are expected to provide their own sheets and towels.

The living room has a stone fireplace and two sets of sliding glass doors to an open deck facing the lake on the north side and a screen porch on the south side. A picture window faces east through woods to the lake. The kitchen has an electric stove, running hot and cold water, a refrigerator, and a dishwasher; it is fully equipped with tableware and utensils.

There is a master bedroom with its own fireplace; a second bedroom with two twin beds. Additionally, there is a small study with an additional twin bed and the living room couch opens into a queen bed. The cottage is insulated for use year round and each room has its own thermostat with electric baseboard heating.

There is an inside shower, an indoor toilet, a small utility closet and a separate space on the side of the cottage with washer and dryer. Garbage must be taken to the main road for municipal collection service.

A rowboat and a canoe go with the cottage. The wharf is located in a side bay and sheltered from the prevailing winds. There is a small sandy area.

Electricity and fireplace wood are included in the usage fee. A telephone is available. The telephone number is (450) 228-2600, and address is: 680 rue Daviault, Entrelacs, Quebec, Canada JOT 2EO.
